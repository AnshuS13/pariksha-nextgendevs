A viral video circulating on social media claims that Mukesh Ambani has announced a massive investment fund exclusively for women entrepreneurs in India. The video also states that a woman named Laila Rao has been appointed to lead this initiative. According to the claim, the fund is expected to support thousands of startups and boost women-led businesses across the country. However, no official announcement has been made by Reliance Industries or Mukesh Ambani regarding such a fund.
