# Impact Model — PARIKSHA AI

## 🎯 Objective

PARIKSHA AI aims to assist journalists, editors, and media organizations in verifying news content efficiently, reducing misinformation, and improving editorial trust.

---

## ⏱️ Time Efficiency

Traditional fact-checking:
- Takes 30–60 minutes per article

With PARIKSHA:
- Verification completed in under 10 seconds

👉 **Time Reduction: ~95%**

---

## 💰 Cost Savings

Manual verification requires:
- Dedicated research teams
- Subscription to verification tools

With PARIKSHA:
- Automated claim extraction + verification

👉 Estimated savings:
- ₹5–10 lakh/year for mid-sized newsroom

---

## ❌ Error Reduction

Manual workflows:
- Prone to human bias
- Risk of missing misleading claims

PARIKSHA:
- Systematically extracts and evaluates multiple claims
- Flags contradictions, weak sources, and bias

👉 **Reduces misinformation risk significantly**

---

## 📊 Decision Support

PARIKSHA provides:
- Credibility score (0–100)
- Claim-level verification
- Source quality analysis
- Bias detection

👉 Enables editors to make faster, data-driven publishing decisions

---

## 🏛️ Auditability & Compliance

- Every verification is stored in SQLite audit logs
- `/history` endpoint provides traceable records

👉 Supports:
- Editorial transparency
- Compliance requirements
- Internal audits

---

## 📈 Scalability

- Can process multiple articles in parallel
- API-based architecture allows integration with newsroom CMS

👉 Ready for enterprise-level deployment

---

## 🌍 Real-World Impact

PARIKSHA can help:
- Reduce spread of fake news
- Improve public trust in journalism
- Support responsible reporting

---

## 🧠 Key Value Proposition

> PARIKSHA is not just a fact-checking tool —  
> it is an intelligent newsroom assistant that combines AI verification, bias detection, and auditability into a single system.
